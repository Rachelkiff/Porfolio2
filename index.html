About me

Hello, I am Rachel Kiffiak. Looking to become an expert Front End Web Developer.
In June of 2020, I decided that it was time for me to make a change in my career and I signed up for a 6 month Full Stack Web Development course through Innotech College in Calgary, AB.
I learnt how to create and deploy a websites. But I found that while I enjoyed learning about the Backend of developing, I enjoyed the Frontend creation more. Its a bit more of a creative process than working on the backend. I completed the course in December of 2020 and recieved my Full Stack Web Development diploma.
In May of 2021, I came across a Dev Ops 3 month course through ComIT and signed up for that as well. We were taught how to set up a pipeline through virtualbox, with Jenkins, Ansible, Docker and Node Js. It was definitely a learning experience. The course was completed in August 2021.
My favorite part of Web Development is that it is constanly changing and it is a constant learning experience.
